My short and long term goals

Today is the 26th of February 2025 and I am 21 years old. When I think about what I want to achieve in the long run, I feel my way of thinking has not changed a lot since I was a child. I have always had the same goal:

Finding a wife whom I can share my life with and create a family. This goes above everything else. In that sense, I have to admit I am very traditional, and I don't doubt it's because of the way I have been raised. Apart from that, I would like to have a stable job where I am comfortable and I enjoy it.

My parents always gave me the impression that working implies doing something you really hate 8 hours a day 5 days a week and being tied to it 11 months per year. Ideally, I would like to avoid that, and find a job where I have more flexibility to work and take breaks whenever I decide, and especially, a job that I enjoy. And that's what I would like to achieve in the next couple of years: learning from people who know more than me. In addition to that, I would like to study a Master's degree.
